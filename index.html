When Gretta’s captivating voice merges with Manny’s instrumental mastery, Royal Soul is born: a duo that fuses elegance and energy in every note. From smooth soul and R&B grooves to vibrant forays into funk, jazz, and pop, their versatile setlist can be tailored to suit any event’s rhythm. They’ve left their mark on gala halls, hotel lobbies, and wedding celebrations around the globe, consistently delivering a sophisticated, life- infused ambiance. Royal Soul doesn’t merely perform songs—they spin musical tales that envelop their audience and turn each gathering into a lasting memory.

With a voice that caresses you from the very first note, Gretta enchants her audience with boundless warmth and versatility. Trained as a lyric singer at the Holguín Conservatory of Music, her expansive repertoire spans classic boleros to sophisticated takes on soul, R&B, funk, pop and jazz each performance colored by her profound interpretive sensitivity. She’s dazzled audiences in Cuba, China, Mexico, and Egypt—her commanding stage presence and uncanny ability to connect with every listener turn each show into an intimate, unforgettable experience. Gretta doesn’t just sing: she weaves atmospheres, stirs emotions, and transforms every song into a unique, magical mome nt .

Manny is the invisible architect behind every melody, breathing life into piano and guitar with equal measures of passion and precision. A graduate of the José White Conservatory in Camagüey, he’s lent his talent to luxury hotels across Russia, China, Mexico, and Cuba, weaving together jazz, bossa nova, funk, and contemporary pop. His gift for improvisation and reinventing beloved tunes makes every performance an unpredictable, thrilling sonic journey. With Manny at the keys or strings, music doesn’t just play—it breathes, grows, and transports listeners to unforgettable soundscapes.
